About Mukami
A psychologist who tells stories

For years I have sat with families in pain — parents who love their children deeply, and children who know their parents care, but neither feels truly understood.

That gap — that silence between them — is what I have dedicated my life to closing. Through clinical practice, life coaching, community programmes, and now through storytelling.

Families don't break because of a lack of love. They break because of a lack of understanding.

Our Mission
Laja Trust

Laja Trust is dedicated to reconciliation in families across Kenya. We believe that when families are restored, communities are transformed. Our work reaches parents, children, and adolescents through counselling, coaching, community programmes, and original storytelling.
