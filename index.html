About Mukami

A clinician who sees what families cannot say

For years I have sat with families who love each other deeply and cannot reach each other. Parents who want to do better and do not know how. Children who have stopped trying to be heard. The distance between them is real, and it grows quietly, in ordinary rooms, during ordinary days.

That gap is what I have dedicated my life to closing. Through clinical practice, life coaching, community programmes, and now through The Kariuki Family, an original animated series that tells the stories families are living but have never seen reflected back to them.

Families do not break because of a lack of love. They break because of patterns inherited before anyone knew to question them. My work begins there.

Our Mission

Laja
Trust

Laja — a Yoruba word meaning to reconcile.

Healed families.
Changed generations.

Laja Trust walks alongside adolescents, youth and families, guiding them from awareness through healing to empowerment, so that the cycles of the past do not become the foundations of the future.

We began in 2018 working with adolescents. Real progress was made, until they went home. Nothing had changed. That observation shaped everything. A young person cannot be healed in isolation from the family system they live inside.

Hope
Most families arrive believing it is too late. We carry hope on their behalf, until they are able to carry it themselves.
Faith
The Word of God is our foundation. Truth is what produces lasting freedom. We draw on both biblical wisdom and evidence-based psychology, because these are not in conflict.
Love
If Christ is the foundation, love is the method. The love that sees people fully, refuses to shame them, and holds firm in the belief that they can be changed.
Adolescents
Our primary focus. Young people navigating family disconnection, identity and patterns they did not choose.
Youth
Young people moving into adulthood, building their own foundations for the first time.
Families
Specifically parents, whose own healing makes a young person's growth sustainable at home.
